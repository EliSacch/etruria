Tasting Experience

Come to meet us, and discover more about our story and products.

The Barrel Room

A tour through the facility that will introduce you to the art of winemaking. After exploring how our wine is crafted we will stop in the barrel room, whit a tasting of our three wines.

Wine Tasting

You can relax in the garden facing our vineyard, where you can enjoy our three wines, accompanied by a tasting board of local products.

The Vineyard Tour

We will guide you throughout our vineyard, to discover how the different variaty of grapes are cultivated. The tour will come to a close to a reserved area amongst the grape vines, with a tasting of our wines.
