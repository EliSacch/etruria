Tudemis

Ruby-red wine, with distinctive red fruit notes.
Dry, full bodied and harmonious, Tudemis embodies the spirit of the soil where it is cultivated

  • Grapes: Sangiovese, Malvasia Nera.
  • Organoleptic test: Intense ruby red color, with aromas of cherry, plum and tobacco. Full bodied and persistent.
  • Alcohol content: 13%
  • Service temperature: 16 - 18°C

Etesiaca

Etesiaca is made of 100% Vermentino, a dry and elegant variaty which has found a perfect terroir in the granite terrain of our lands.

  • Grapes: Vermentino.
  • Organoleptic test: Limpid, hay-yellow color, with green tones. Fruity bouquet with hints of citrus, acacia and pinapple.
  • Alcohol content: 11%
  • Service temperature: 6 - 8°C

Apiana

Aromatic wine, well known for its amber color and signature sweetness.
Smooth and full bodied, Apiana is a perfect match for your desserts.

  • Grapes: Moscato.
  • Organoleptic test: Intense amber color, with aromas of honey, peach and vanilla. Full bodied and persistent.
  • Alcohol content: 13%
  • Service temperature: 8 - 10°C
