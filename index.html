Our Family

Etruria was born out of the dream of Bruno, who inherited these lands from his grandparents in the 1966.
The art of winemaking was transmitted through the generations, to his chidren first and grandchildren then.
Elia, Paolo and Andrea run the family business now, and they dedicate all their passion and efforts to keep alive the traditions, which Bruno had thought them.

The vineyard

The vineyard is situated in Tuscany, the hearth of the territory once called Etruria, and the cultivated area extends on a surface of 2 hectars.
Planted with Sangiovese, Malvasia Nera, Vermentino and Moscato, the composition of the terroir allows the grapes to maintain their unique variety of flavours, fruit of the great history of these lands.
